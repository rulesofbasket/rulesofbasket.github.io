6 Most Important Rules in Basketball

1. Traveling

Players cannot take more than two steps without dribbling the ball.

2. Double Dribble

A player cannot dribble the ball, stop, and then start dribbling again.

3. Fouls

Players must avoid illegal physical contact with opponents.

4. Shot Clock

Teams must attempt a shot within a specified time frame.

5. Three-Point Line

Shots made from beyond the three-point line count for three points.

6. Out of Bounds

The ball is out of bounds when it touches the ground outside the boundary lines.
